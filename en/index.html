Who we are

High-quality legal services for entrepreneurs, companies and private clients

We advise and litigate in complex matters of corporate law, contractual disputes, shareholder conflicts, family law, inheritance law and wealth issues. In doing so, we guide clients not only legally, but also think strategically and fiscally about structures, transactions and lasting solutions.

Our clients expect more than a legal advisor alone. They look for lawyers who understand interests, foresee risks and act with discretion, conviction and precision.

The name Shield symbolises the core of our practice: protecting, safeguarding and strengthening the interests of our clients. Shield Advocaten stands alongside entrepreneurs, companies and families for the long term and safeguards their legal, strategic and commercial interests, at every stage of entrepreneurship, growth, transaction or conflict.

We protect what is of value to our clients.

Trusted advisors for entrepreneurs

Lawyers who understand entrepreneurship

Successful entrepreneurs need lawyers who understand entrepreneurship. Who can act quickly, grasp complex interests and deliver solutions that make sense legally, strategically and commercially.

Shield Advocaten guides entrepreneurs, shareholders, directors and companies in negotiations, governance issues, international transactions, liability, contracts and strategic conflicts.

We also advise on corporate structures, the incorporation of companies, asset protection, tax matters and the legal organisation of businesses and investments, always with a focus on continuity, growth and risk management.

We regularly act as a sparring partner for directors and shareholders in strategic choices, restructurings, conflicts and far-reaching measures within companies.

In addition, Shield Advocaten offers, to a limited extent, guidance and coaching for directors and entrepreneurs. In specific situations we can provide temporary support within a company when managerial continuity requires it, for example when a director drops out or during a transition period.

We act pragmatically where possible and litigate sharply where necessary.
