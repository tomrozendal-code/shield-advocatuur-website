Family law

Shield Advocatuur advises on resolving problems in the personal sphere.

Divorce and visitation arrangements

This may involve a divorce and everything that comes with it, such as a visitation arrangement for the children, a parenting plan and maintenance.

But matters such as acknowledgement of paternity and custody of a child, a supervision order or the out-of-home placement of a child are also family-law issues on which Shield Advocatuur can advise and support you.

Youth law and supervision orders

Shield Advocatuur can assist you in proceedings against the Child Care and Protection Board or against one of the certified institutions / the youth protection officer.
