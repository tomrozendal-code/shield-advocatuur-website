Criminal Law

Shield Advocatuur can assist you when you, as an individual or entrepreneur, face criminal prosecution. Shield Advocatuur can also help you prevent criminal prosecution.

Most entrepreneurs will think: criminal law has nothing to do with me, and fortunately that is generally the case.

Your business and criminal law

Regularly, unfortunately, things are different; consider, for example, situations in which you discover that matters are going on within your business that you had no or insufficient oversight of and/or were unaware of.

A bookkeeper who unintentionally makes an incorrect entry, leaving the tax authorities disadvantaged? You never knew about it, but as a director you can certainly be held responsible.

Prevent prosecution, get in touch immediately

Should it come your way, do not wait to engage a specialist but contact Shield Advocatuur immediately, so that prosecution may perhaps be prevented.
