Compliance

Governments, banks, accountants and business partners expect more and more from individuals and businesses when it comes to compliance. Non-compliance can have serious consequences, for the individual (UBO), the organisation and for directors and managers personally.

Thorough guidance with complex compliance requirements

Shield Advocatuur is your partner in complying with applicable laws and regulations. With experience and knowledge across various areas of law, Shield Advocatuur helps individuals and businesses meet complex compliance requirements.

Experience shows that high-net-worth individuals and businesses regularly encounter the same compliance questions at different organisations, which can cause frustration.

Shield Advocatuur is glad to help make this process as smooth, efficient and discreet as possible.

Compliance & Integrity

When it comes to compliance, you can rely on sound guidance, support and expertise.

Examples of laws and rules that almost all businesses deal with include:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Competition Act (Mededingingswet)
  • Anti-Money Laundering and Anti-Terrorist Financing Act (Wwft)
  • Environmental Management Act (Wet milieubeheer)
  • Foreign Nationals (Employment) Act (Wav)
  • Security Screening of Investments, Mergers and Acquisitions Act (Vifo Act)
  • The Environment and Planning Act (Omgevingswet)
