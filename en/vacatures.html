Vacancy

Legal secretary

Do you want to work in a dynamic and professional environment where you get every chance to develop yourself? We are looking for a legal secretary.

The duties include, among other things

  • Handling incoming and outgoing correspondence
  • Complex calendar management and monitoring deadlines
  • Preparing litigation documents
  • File management (creating clients/files, conflict checks, preparing engagement letters)
  • KYC/Wwft administration (gathering data)
  • Preparing invoices

In addition, you provide support where needed and flag bottlenecks, so that the lawyer can focus fully on their core activity. No two days are the same, and the variety of tasks makes this a versatile role.

Shield Advocatuur is looking for an enthusiastic and flexible colleague who knows how to get things done. You handle pressure well and stay accurate, even in stressful situations. You are a real team player, but can certainly also work well independently.
