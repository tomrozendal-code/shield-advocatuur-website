About us

A boutique firm with the strength of a large one

Shield Advocaten is an independent boutique law firm that protects, supports and strengthens entrepreneurs, investors and families — with the depth of a large firm and the personal dedication of a niche practice.

Boutique law firm

Who we are

Shield Advocaten was founded on a clear conviction: that the finest legal service arises not from size, but from commitment. We are an independent boutique firm — small enough to truly stand beside our clients, and sharp enough to master the most complex matters.

The name Shield is no coincidence. A shield protects, guards and strengthens; that is precisely what we do for our clients’ interests. At every stage of enterprise, growth, transaction or conflict, we stand at their side — with discretion, conviction and precision.

Our lawyers combine the depth and analytical power of a top-tier firm with the agility, speed and personal involvement only a boutique can offer. No anonymous files, but a dedicated, consistent team that knows and safeguards your interests.

We deliver the quality of a large firm, at the rates and with the dedication of a niche practice.

We protect what is of value to our clients.

Our clients

Who we are here for

Our clients are diverse, yet share one thing: they expect more than advice alone. They seek a partner who understands interests, foresees risks and acts — whether the matter is a multi-million transaction or a family question.

Major projects & private equity

We guide investors, private equity houses and companies through complex transactions, acquisitions, restructurings and governance matters — cross-border where needed, and with an eye for every detail that makes the difference.

International companies in the Netherlands

Companies entering the Dutch market from abroad find in Shield their steady legal guide. We assist with the incorporation and structuring of companies, contracts, compliance and governance — serving as the local legal anchor for those doing business in the Netherlands from abroad.

The SME entrepreneur

For the entrepreneur in small and medium-sized business, Shield is a steady legal backbone. From contracts and compliance to disputes and growth, we think along as a sparring partner and act decisively where needed — accessible, and without the barriers of a large firm.

Private wealth & families

Affluent families entrust us with their most personal and valuable interests. We advise on wealth structuring, succession, family businesses and family-law matters — with the discretion and care this field demands, across generations.
