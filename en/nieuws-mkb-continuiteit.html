Continuity for the entrepreneur: steering an SME through a contractual dispute

For an SME, a single dispute can put continuity under pressure. Shield Advocaten stood by an entrepreneur cornered by a supplier — swiftly, practically and tenaciously.

The situation

A mid-sized company was confronted with a supplier that failed to meet its obligations while at the same time demanding payment. Deliveries stalled, cash flow came under pressure and the relationship with the company’s own customers threatened to suffer. For an SME, such a situation quickly becomes threatening.

Our approach

Shield Advocaten first assessed the contractual position and the underlying terms. We served notice of default on the supplier, conducted firm negotiations and prepared proceedings in parallel — so that our client could operate from a position of strength rather than uncertainty.

For an SME, what counts is not only being right, but above all the speed with which that is achieved.

We kept the entrepreneur closely involved throughout and deliberately chose a pragmatic route where we could, and a firm one where we had to.

The outcome

The dispute was resolved within a few weeks through an arrangement that compensated the damage suffered and restored the deliveries. The company retained its customers, its cash flow and its peace of mind.
