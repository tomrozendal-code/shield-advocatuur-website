Cookie policy

In the cookie policy you can read which cookies Shield Advocatuur uses and for what purpose.

Last updated: 22 July 2026.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that may be placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar techniques include, for example, pixels and local storage. Cookies can be placed by the website itself (first-party) or by a third party whose content is shown on the page (third-party).

2. Which cookies do we use?

Shield Advocatuur does not itself place cookies to track visitors or for advertising purposes. Our website uses only:

  • Functional and necessary cookies — required for the website to work properly and to remember your preferences (such as the selected language). No consent is required for these cookies.
  • Third-party services — to display fonts (Google Fonts) and icons (jsDelivr), files are retrieved from external parties, which may involve your IP address being passed to those parties. On our contact page we also show an embedded Google Maps map; Google may place cookies and process data in accordance with Google’s own privacy policy.

3. Consent

No consent is required for functional and necessary cookies. Cookies that may be placed by embedded third-party services (such as Google Maps) only become active when you visit the relevant page or content. Because we do not place any statistical, tracking or marketing cookies ourselves, we do not show a cookie banner. If you wish to prevent Google from placing cookies via the embedded map, you can avoid the contact page or block third-party cookies in your browser.

4. Managing and deleting cookies

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to notify you before a cookie is placed. Disabling cookies may cause certain parts of the website to work less well. Please consult your browser’s help function for the exact procedure.

5. Changes and contact

We may amend this cookie policy from time to time. The most recent version is always available on this page. If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at contact@shieldadvocatuur.nl or +31 (0)35 7850717. See also our privacy policy.
