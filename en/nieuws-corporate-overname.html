A strategic acquisition without surprises: advising an internationally operating company

In a cross-border acquisition, success stands or falls on preparation. Shield Advocaten guided an internationally operating company from first exploration to closing.

The mandate

An internationally operating company wished to make a strategic acquisition of an industry peer with activities in several countries. The stakes were high: a misjudgement of liabilities, contracts or compliance obligations could damage the transaction — and the reputation behind it.

Our approach

Shield Advocaten carried out focused due diligence and brought the legal risks into sharp relief: from existing agreements and governance to competition and compliance matters. We then translated those findings to the negotiating table, with robust warranties, indemnities and conditions precedent.

In a transaction, it is not the price that makes the difference, but what lies beneath it.

We coordinated the advisers involved, kept watch over the timeline and ensured that the company knew exactly where it stood at every moment.

The outcome

The acquisition was completed successfully, within the set timeframe and with a risk allocation that protected our client. Integration could begin on a legal footing that was firmly in order.
